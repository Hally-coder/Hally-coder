# MockMate — AI Interview Platform

MockMate is an AI-powered interview platform that helps recruiters conduct structured candidate screening at scale. Recruiters can create an interview from a job description, invite candidates through secure links, and review structured evaluations after completion.

> The production source code is private. This page is a code-free product showcase.

## Live product

- [Visit the MockMate website](https://mockmate-site.vercel.app)
- [Open the product demo](https://mockmate-ai-tau.vercel.app)

## Candidate experience

- Secure invitation-based interview access
- Camera and microphone pre-checks
- Interview rules and candidate consent
- Timed questions with automatic progression
- Desktop fullscreen integrity controls
- Continuous interview-session recording
- Recovery support for interrupted sessions
- Automatic completion when the interview timer expires

## Recruiter experience

- Candidate and interview creation
- AI-generated questions based on job descriptions
- Secure shareable invitation links
- Structured scoring and evaluation comments
- Candidate results dashboard
- PDF interview reports
- Report delivery support
- Interview integrity monitoring

## Technology overview

- Next.js, React, and TypeScript
- Modern responsive interface
- Secure authentication
- AI-assisted question generation and evaluation
- Database-backed interview workflows
- Browser-based media recording
- Payment integration
- Vercel deployment

## Product direction

MockMate is designed to reduce repetitive first-round screening while keeping hiring evaluations structured and reviewable. Future directions include ATS integrations, client portals, multi-tenant SaaS capabilities, and historical benchmarking.
